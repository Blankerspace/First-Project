My favourite meal is Fried Rice with vegetable salad

You can say I love green meals, lol


Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried

wok or a frying pan and is usually mixed with other ingredients

such as eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meat.

It is often eaten by itself or as an accompaniment to another dish.


Ingredient Measurement
Rice 1 Cup
Vegetable oil 2 Tsp
Eggs 2 pcs
Bacon rashers 2 chopped pcs
Carrot 1pcs peeled and grated
Trimmed Shallots 2pcs
Frozen peas 1/2 cup
Soy sauce 1 Tbsp

Method to prepare the meal

  • Cook the rice in a saucepan of boiling water for 12 mins
  • Drain the water and leave to cool
  • Heat oil in non-stick wok or large frying pan over medium heat
  • Add eggs and swirl over base to form an omelette
  • Cook for 2 minutes or until set
  • Transfer to a chopping board, set aside to cool and cut into short strips
  • Add bacon to wok, cook for 4 minutes and add carrots
  • Stir fry for a minute, add shallots, peas and rice
  • Cook, stirring, 3-4 minutes, add egg and soy sauce, stir until heated through
  • Sprinkle with sesame seeds and top with extra shallots
  • Serve immediately
